Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. Here's how we protect it.

Last Updated: November 29, 2025

Our Privacy Promise

Simplicity is built with privacy at its core. Your tasks stay on your device. We don't collect, read, store, or sell your personal data. What you do with Simplicity is your business, not ours.

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Simplicity ("we," "our," or "us") handles information when you use our mobile application ("App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

By using Simplicity, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our App.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

We do not collect any personal information. Simplicity is designed to work entirely on your device without requiring user accounts, email addresses, phone numbers, or any other personal identifiers.

Task Data

All task data you create in Simplicity is stored locally on your device using SQLite database technology. This includes:

  • Task titles and descriptions
  • Task creation and completion timestamps
  • Task status (active, completed, or archived)
  • Task expiration times

This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to back up your device. We have no access to your tasks and cannot read, modify, or delete them.

Usage Analytics

We do not collect usage analytics, crash reports, or any telemetry data. We do not track how you use the App, which features you interact with, or when you use the App.

Device Information

We do not collect any device information such as device ID, operating system version, device model, or mobile network information.

How We Use Information

Since we don't collect any information, we don't use any information. Your task data remains on your device and is used solely by the App to provide its core functionality:

  • Display your active tasks
  • Track task completion and expiration
  • Generate completion statistics
  • Maintain your task archive
  • Send local notifications (if you grant permission)

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using industry-standard SQLite database technology. This data is protected by your device's built-in security features, including:

  • Device encryption (if enabled on your device)
  • Device passcode/biometric authentication
  • App sandboxing (data isolation from other apps)

No Cloud Storage

We do not use any cloud services to store your data. There are no remote servers, no cloud backups, and no data synchronization. Your data exists only on your physical device.

Device Backups

If you use iCloud Backup (iOS) or Google Drive Backup (Android), your task data may be included in your device backup. This is controlled by your device's operating system, not by Simplicity. We recommend reviewing your device's backup settings if you have privacy concerns.

Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not share any data with third parties because we don't collect any data.

No Third-Party Services

Simplicity does not integrate with or use any third-party services, including:

  • No analytics services (e.g., Google Analytics, Mixpanel)
  • No advertising networks
  • No crash reporting services
  • No social media integrations
  • No tracking or marketing tools

No Data Sales

We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to third parties. This has always been our policy and always will be.

App Permissions

Simplicity requests minimal permissions to function:

Notifications (Optional)

If you grant notification permissions, the App can send you local notifications when tasks are about to expire. These notifications are generated entirely on your device and do not involve any external servers or services.

Storage Access

The App requires access to your device's local storage to save your task data. This storage is private to the App and cannot be accessed by other applications.

You can revoke any permissions at any time through your device settings. Revoking permissions may limit certain features of the App.

Your Rights and Control

Data Access

All your data is accessible directly within the App. You can view, edit, and delete your tasks at any time.

Data Deletion

You have complete control over your data:

  • Delete individual tasks by swiping or using the delete function
  • Clear your entire archive from the Archive screen
  • Uninstall the App to permanently delete all local data

Once deleted from your device, the data is permanently removed and cannot be recovered (unless backed up through your device's backup system).

Data Portability

Since all your data is stored locally, you maintain full control and ownership. Future versions of Simplicity may include export functionality to allow you to transfer your data to other applications or formats.

Children's Privacy

Simplicity does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. Since we don't collect any personal information from anyone, the App is safe for users of all ages. However, we recommend parental supervision for children using any mobile application.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Posting a notice within the App
  • Providing prominent notice on our website

Your continued use of Simplicity after any changes indicates your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

International Users

Simplicity is available worldwide. Since all data is stored locally on your device and we don't collect any information, there are no international data transfers or cross-border privacy concerns.

Users in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, and other regions with data protection regulations can use Simplicity with confidence knowing that no personal data is collected, processed, or transferred.

Legal Compliance

GDPR Compliance (European Users)

Because we don't collect personal data, most GDPR requirements don't apply to Simplicity. However, we respect the principles of GDPR including data minimization, purpose limitation, and user control.

CCPA Compliance (California Users)

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), we want to be clear:

  • We do not sell your personal information
  • We do not collect personal information
  • We do not share personal information with third parties

Other Regional Laws

We strive to comply with privacy laws in all jurisdictions where Simplicity is available. Our privacy-first approach exceeds the requirements of most data protection regulations.

Privacy Policy Summary

In short: Simplicity doesn't collect, store, or share any of your personal information. Your tasks stay on your device. We have no servers, no analytics, no tracking, and no third-party integrations. Your privacy is absolute.
